About

I'm Doktor! I'm a photographer and college student living in Boston, MA. In my free time, I photograph big animals.

Contact info and other useful links can be found on my other website.

Photos

You can find my photos on this website, and on Twitter and Flickr! Flickr serves as a mirror for this website. Additionally, I keep track of my work on Trello, which I update frequently.

Site

The backend of this site is written in Python 3 with Django. The photo gallery runs on PhotoSwipe, written by Dmitry Semenov.

If you notice anything that's broken or working improperly, please let me know! This includes broken links, improperly displayed photos, etc. General feedback is also appreciated.
