About me

I'm Doktor! I'm 21. I live in Boston, MA, USA. In my free time, I photograph big animals.

I post random photos and dumb thoughts on Twitter. You can find links to all my social media profiles on my other website, doktorthehusky.com.

If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to send me a message on Twitter: @DoktorTheHusky, or send me an email: doktorthehusky@gmail.com.

Photography

I'm self-taught — I've never had any formal training in photography. There's a list of resources I recommend at the bottom of this page. The most important thing you can do is take lots of photos!

I don't specialize in any specific photography disciplines, but I particularly enjoy portraits, landscapes, and events.

History

In September 2015, I picked up a DSLR for the first time. That was my senior year of high school; I took photos for the yearbook.

In January 2016, I attended my first convention, Anthro New England 2016. I volunteered in the Photo Room and took fursuit photos for the first time. As the saying goes, the rest is history.

My first camera was my parents' Canon EOS 550D. In June 2016, I switched to a Canon EOS 80D. In October 2017, I switched to my current camera, a Canon EOS 5D Mark IV.

Anthro New England

I'm a staff member for Anthro New England (ANE), a convention held in Boston, MA. I'm part of the Photography Department. I organize and run the Photo Room, the Fursuit Group Photo, and the Fursuit "Parade". I volunteered in the Photo Room in 2016, and started organizing and running it in 2017.

Commercial work

I don't often take on private shoots or projects, but get in touch if you have ideas.

If you want to support my work, check out my Ko-fi page!
