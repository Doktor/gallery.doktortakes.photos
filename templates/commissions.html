Commissions

I offer photoshoot commissions!

Pricing

$40 per 30-minute timeslot, maximum 2 slots per commissioner.
Post-processing is included for free.
Tips are appreciated!
Prices are subject to change.

Payment is accepted by cash or PayPal, any time before the photoshoot.

Content

We'll discuss the content of the photoshoot before it happens. If you have a theme in mind, we can work with that; we can also discuss specific poses and ideas.

Examples

  • Show off a fursuit
  • Scenery, nature, landscapes: if the convention center is near those
  • Action: dance, martial arts, etc.
  • Holiday themed
  • Bedroom photos

The shoot

You should have a friend to accompany you as a handler, and to carry your belongings, particularly badges.

Times are flexible: we may end early or late, depending on how many ideas we have.

Photos

Photos will be delivered 2–8 weeks after the photoshoot. I'll notify you in advance if I expect it to take longer. You'll receive two sets of JPG files:

  • 2400x1600 with watermark, to post and share on the Internet
  • 3600x2400 without watermark, for personal use

The files are free for you to use in any non-commercial settings. You can post and share them, make prints for yourself, etc., but you cannot sell them in any form without a prior agreement with me.

If you need the photos in different dimensions or ratios, please ask!

I will not sell or otherwise release my RAW files.

Availability

I offer 3-5 photoshoots per convention! You can see my availability on Trello.

Scheduling

If you're interested, get in touch! Twitter messages or emails are preferred.
